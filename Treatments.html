Treatment

ADHD Test/Treatment

At Clinic for Kids, we ensure that your child is tested upon request as soon as possible.

Developmental Pediatrics

We have specialists who are able to help children who have a unique or deficient level of mental growth.

Premature Birth

We have specialists who are trained to help anyone who is in need of care if they have a premature infant, as they need to continue developing.

Pediatric Care

We provide medical assistance for anyone between the ages of 0-18 years old. We believe that universal healthcare should be offered to all and therefore will be willing to help.

Consultation With Parents/Children

If you are ever struggling with any medical or school-related situation, we are here to help you out.

Constipation

Feeling stomach pain? Well, that may be a symptom of constipation. We are here to help people of varying ages with this issue through treatments.

Growth Abnormality

We have specialists who are willing to consult with the parent and child about their abnormality of growth.

Behavior

If you are in need of a consultation with a pediatrician, we are willing to talk to the child and have a form of "therapy".

Vaccines

We provide a consultation with the guardian of the child to ensure which vaccines are government-mandated, as well as which are optimal for the child.

Learning Issues

If your child has problems with learning, we are here to help by consulting with the students to better understand how to fix their issues, along with their parents.
